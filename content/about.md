I'm Chris Zinda, a Senior Technical Product Manager at Red Hat with over 15 years of experience in IT security, identity management, and enterprise infrastructure.

## What I Do

I serve as Product Manager for **Red Hat Certificate System**, **Red Hat Directory Server**, and **Identity Management**. My work focuses on shaping the product vision for enterprise identity and security platforms, partnering with engineering teams to architect secure software solutions, and driving cross-functional initiatives across engineering, documentation, and support teams.

## Areas of Expertise

- **Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)** - Deep technical and business understanding of certificate management, lifecycle automation, and trust architectures
- **Identity and Access Management (IAM)** - Design, implementation, and maintenance of enterprise identity solutions
- **Zero Trust Architecture** - Building security frameworks that verify every access request
- **Product Management** - Translating customer needs and market insights into product strategy and roadmap priorities

## Background

Before moving into product management, I spent over 8 years as a Senior Technical Account Manager at Red Hat, specializing in PKI and two-factor authentication as an Identity and Access Management expert.

Prior to Red Hat, I worked on PKI initiatives for the Department of Defense, ensuring FIPS 140-2 compliance and implementing certificate status protocols for DISA Operations Group. My career began in systems administration and engineering roles, where I built expertise in Linux environments, automation, and infrastructure security.
